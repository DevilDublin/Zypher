Where Zypher Agent quietly steps in.

We focus on moments where speed, tone and accuracy matter – and where a missed message usually means a missed opportunity.

First-contact and lead capture

AI agents that greet visitors, ask sensible questions, qualify intent and route only serious opportunities to you or your team.

  • Website and landing page entry points
  • WhatsApp and SMS hand-offs
  • Automatic lead scoring into your CRM

Appointment setting and routing

Agents that negotiate times, check calendars and send confirmations, so you stop going back-and-forth over email.

  • Two-way calendar synchronisation
  • Reschedule and cancellation flows
  • Routing by team, service or location

Operational follow-up and admin

Behind-the-scenes workflows that update records, send follow-ups and keep everyone in the loop after each interaction.

  • Call summaries into Slack or email
  • Automatic status updates in your CRM
  • Alerts if anything fails or looks unusual
