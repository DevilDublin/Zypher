Voice agent

Universal inbound booking and triage

A neutral, sector-agnostic voice agent that answers inbound calls, understands why the person is calling, and either books time in a diary or routes them to the right place.

Useful for: consultants, agencies, service providers Outcome: booked slots, fewer missed calls
Chat assistant

Website intake and qualification assistant

A conversational assistant that lives on your website, gathers context, checks fit and then passes the right opportunities on to you with a neatly structured summary.

⌨

Example conversation:

Visitor: “Hi, we’re looking for help automating parts of our onboarding.”
Assistant: “Happy to help. Roughly how many new clients do you onboard each month?”
Visitor: “Between 20 and 30.”
Assistant: “Understood. Are you mainly looking to automate scheduling, document collection, or something else?”
Visitor: “Scheduling and reminders to chase documents.”
Assistant: “That makes sense. I’ll share this with the team along with your email so they can suggest a sensible starting point.”

Channels: on-site widget, WhatsApp, SMS Outcome: fewer form fills, more real conversations
Voice agent

Missed-call rescue and call-back agent

When you miss a call, the agent can automatically dial back, politely acknowledge the missed call and either capture a message or secure the next step.

▶

Example scenario:

Agent: “Hello, this is the automated assistant calling back on behalf of the team. I can help you leave a message or book a call. Which would you prefer?”
Caller: “I’d like to book a call for tomorrow afternoon.”
Agent: “No problem. I can see availability at 2pm or 4pm. Which works better?”
Caller: “4pm, please.”
Agent: “You’re all booked for 4pm tomorrow. You’ll receive a confirmation shortly.”

Useful for: solo founders, lean teams Outcome: fewer lost opportunities when you’re busy
Automation flow

Post-call summary and follow-up sequence

After a call or chat, an automation flow drafts a summary, updates the correct record and prepares a follow-up email so nothing slips through the cracks.

⚙

Example output:

– Contact: Jane Smith
– Context: Interested in a done-for-you automation package
– Key points: wants to reduce manual data entry, currently using Google Sheets and HubSpot
– Next step: 45-minute scoping call next Wednesday

Draft follow-up email and updated CRM record created automatically.

Integrations: calendar, CRM, email Outcome: consistent follow-up without extra admin
Internal assistant

Internal operations and “ask me anything” agent

An internal assistant that understands your own documentation, playbooks and processes, so your team can ask questions and receive clear answers in seconds.

⌨

Example exchange:

Team member: “What’s our standard process for onboarding a new client on the premium plan?”
Assistant: “There are five steps: 1) Collect key details, 2) Set up workspace, 3) Connect tools, 4) Test the flow, 5) Book the onboarding call. I can paste the full checklist if you’d like.”
Team member: “Paste the checklist and link me to the template, please.”
Assistant: “Here’s the checklist and the link to the current onboarding template in Notion.”

Useful for: onboarding, support, operations Outcome: fewer “quick questions” interrupting your day
